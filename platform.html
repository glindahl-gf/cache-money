The Greenfly Arc

Humans Create. Greenfly Distributes. Collects. Identifies. Tags. Organizes. Distributes. Measures. Automates.

01

Collect.

Every source. Every format. One pipeline.

Professional creators in the field — 4K uploads from the sideline in real time. Automated feeds from broadcast partners. DAM integrations. Fan submissions. Content enters Greenfly the moment it's created — whether a photographer uploads from the sideline or an integration pulls from an existing system.

02

Organize.

Identify, tag, and organize content the way your team does. Faster.

Content enters the system. RosterIQ identifies players by face and jersey number. BrandIQ detects every sponsor logo. SceneIQ classifies the context. Routing rules deliver assets to the right gallery based on source, creator, or tags — configured logic that runs alongside the AI, not after it.

The IQ Suite

Two kinds of intelligence, one system.

RosterIQ Player Recognition

Identifies players by face and jersey number across image and video — the core AI differentiator. A player photographed from behind is still identifiable by number; a face in the frame is still identifiable when the jersey isn't visible. The identity layer that makes personalized Galleries possible.

BrandIQ Sponsor & Logo Detection

The revenue layer. Detects sponsor logos across image and video, routing content directly to brand partners and documenting every appearance — connecting content distribution to sponsorship proof-of-performance.

SceneIQ Scene Recognition

Distinguishes game action from practice drill, press conference from celebration, red carpet from meet-and-greet. Context-aware routing without manual sorting.

03

Distribute.

The right content. Every relevant stakeholder.

Approval workflows clear content for distribution. AI and routing rules work together to deliver every asset to the right person, the right gallery, the right channel — without manual intervention.

04

Activate.

Every relevant stakeholder. Simultaneous activation.

Athletes, talent, partners, sponsors, affiliates — every person and property in your orbit has an audience. Most are underserved. Greenfly connects each stakeholder to relevant content automatically — the right assets, the right channels, without manual intervention. The network you already have starts performing like one.

05

Measure.

Arm your team with the receipts they deserve.

Track assets from genesis to external channel. Impressions. Engagements. Utilization trends. Measurable earned media value. Proof you can pull up and point to.

06

Automate.

Humans Create. Greenfly Automates.

The entire arc — collect, identify, tag, organize, distribute, measure — runs without manual intervention. The people who make the content focus on making the content. Everything else is infrastructure.
