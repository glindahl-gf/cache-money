Origin

The reason we built it.

“We have over 900 MLB players on our platform, and when Shawn was at spring training players kept coming up to him and saying: ‘I love Greenfly — I use it every day.’”

Daniel Kirschner, CEO & Co-Founder

Spring 2014. Two-time MLB All-Star Shawn Green wanted a faster way to capture and share videos from his phone — to answer the steady stream of questions about his storied career. He had a hunch the idea was a home run, and asked Daniel Kirschner — a digital-media exec — to weigh in. They prototyped the first version of Greenfly in Santa Monica that spring.

A decade later, Greenfly powers daily content operations for hundreds of the most-watched leagues, broadcasters, teams and brands on Earth. The mission hasn't changed: fuel fandom by unleashing the power of short-form digital media.

The full team→
Co-Founder & CEO
Daniel Kirschner

Daniel Kirschner, CEO and co-founder of Greenfly, is an innovative entrepreneur transforming how the world of sports and entertainment engages their audiences. A Harvard Law graduate and former President of the Harvard Law Review, Daniel previously advised the U.S. Department of Justice, the FCC, and led corporate strategy at Activision Blizzard. At Greenfly, he has pioneered a platform trusted by MLB, NBA, NFL, Premier League, and global brands to drive explosive digital growth through authentic content that engages fans wherever they are watching. Daniel's leadership continues to shape the future of short-form content, setting new standards for live content operations, digital fan engagement and commercial opportunities through digital content.

Co-Founder & Chairman
Shawn Green

Shawn Green played Major League Baseball for 15 years. He was named an All-Star for two seasons; he holds notable hitting records and recorded still-unbeaten achievements. A lifelong passionate fan of technology, Shawn developed the Greenfly concept out of his desire to streamline the complexities of the media creation experience with athletes, and help them share and amplify digital videos and photos across their channels. Prior to playing professional baseball, Shawn attended Stanford University.

Chief Operating Officer
Sammi Marwan

Sammi Marwan, Chief Operating Officer at Greenfly, is a corporate strategist focused on driving growth and operational excellence. With more than 15 years of experience leading transformations across media, entertainment, and sports, Sammi ensures Greenfly operates at peak efficiency while scaling globally. At Greenfly, he designs the strategies and workflows that unlock profitability, enable new products and revenue lines, and power expansion across the globe. Prior to Greenfly, Sammi served as Vice President of Strategy, Operations & Growth at Endeavor/TKO (including IMG and 160over90), where he built the firm's strategic consulting practice and redefined operating models across multiple business divisions.

Chief Technology Officer
Marshall Greer

Marshall is a technology executive with over 15 years of experience leading dynamic engineering teams. As CTO, he manages the technical strategy and execution of Greenfly's product, managed services, and business initiatives. Prior to Greenfly, Marshall led engineering at Stella & Dot where he defined the overall architecture and design of a next-generation social selling platform that powered more than $200M in annual global sales. He has a passion for building and managing world-class engineering teams and guiding them with innovative methods and new technologies to deliver reliable and scalable products that amaze customers.

Chief Product Officer
Rosanne Hong

Rosanne is a product leader with over 15 years of experience working across the tech landscape. As the head of product at Greenfly, Rosanne partners directly with customers and customer success to deeply understand customer problems and works closely with the product, design and engineering teams to discover and deliver solutions. She is passionate about product development and believes there are always ways to improve upon the status quo. Prior to Greenfly, Rosanne has had a range of experiences across game studios, including Electronic Arts, Playtika and Activision Blizzard, as well as various media and web properties at Demand Media and Internet Brands. Rosanne graduated from UCLA with a BS in Computer Science.

Chief Revenue Officer
Mark Keaney

Mark Keaney leads the global sales and customer success teams at Greenfly. He is a veteran sales leader with a proven track record and history of success at some of the biggest brands in sports and media (CBS, Viacom, LPGA, and Comcast). He previously headed North American sales at Khoros, where he helped brands harness the power of digital channels and social media. He has led sales teams at enterprise SaaS companies like Sprinklr as well as early-stage firms like SocialSphere and GMBi. The key to his success has been building incredible teams that share a unified vision, inspired enthusiasm, and embrace a customer-first mission. Together with clear thought leadership, his teams always have a picture of what success looks like and a roadmap to get there. Mark has also worked independently as a sales consultant, inspiring clients to "brand globally, but market locally". He's an avid golfer and sports fan, and has also authored a book and launched a non-profit organization for kids at risk. Mark graduated from Boston College and majored in economics with a concentration in marketing.

Managing Director, EMEA
Nick Shaw

Nick has over 20 years of leadership experience across sports and digital media. Prior to joining Greenfly, Nick served as the Lead of Sports Teams, Leagues and Federations at Meta where he focused on partnerships across EMEA including UEFA Champions League, UEFA Men's and Women's Euros, FIFA Men's and Women's World Cup, Rugby World Cup, Cricket World Cup and the Olympics. Prior to Meta, he held various senior roles instrumental to building digital capabilities at sports and broadcast properties, including serving as the Head of Digital at Rugby Football Union, Head of Sport at Now TV (Sky) and the Head of Digital at the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB).

GM, Greenfly AI

As General Manager of the AI group at Greenfly, Evan oversees a team dedicated to the creation and support of some of the industry's most advanced sports centric AI initiatives. Having lived in a wide array of locations, from Chicago to Singapore, Evan now resides in Nashville, TN.
